Discover SETU & Waterford,
Your Gateway to a Vibrant Music Scene

SETU is located in the heart of Waterford, a vibrant and historic city known for its rich cultural heritage and thriving arts scene.

By choosing to study music at SETU in Waterford, you'll be immersing yourself in a city with a rich musical tradition. From the internationally renowned All Together Now festival to the many live music venues dotted throughout the city, there are countless opportunities to get involved in the local music scene and gain valuable experience.

Whether you're a local or from further afield, Waterford offers plenty of opportunities for fun and exploration, from its beautiful coastline to its bustling streets and lively pubs and nightclubs. We believe that studying music at SETU is not just an education, but an experience that will stay with you for a lifetime.
